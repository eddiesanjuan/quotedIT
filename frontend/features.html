🏠 Dashboard

Your dashboard shows a quick overview of your quoting activity:

  • Quotes this month - Total quotes created
  • Total value - Combined value of all quotes
  • Pending quotes - Quotes awaiting customer response
  • Win rate - Percentage of quotes that convert to jobs

Use these stats to track your business growth and identify patterns.

Create quotes without clicking - just tap the microphone and describe your job. The AI understands natural language like:

  • "New quote for John Smith - painting the living room, about 400 square feet, needs primer and two coats"
  • "Quote for furniture assembly at 123 Main St - two IKEA wardrobes and a bookshelf"
  • "Bathroom remodel for Mary Johnson - replace vanity, install new faucet, and retile the shower floor"

The more detail you provide, the more accurate your quote will be.

Your dashboard includes a task list to help you stay organized:

  • Follow-up reminders - Never forget to follow up on pending quotes
  • Job deadlines - Track when work is due
  • Custom tasks - Add your own to-dos

Tasks can be marked complete with a single tap.

See your recent quotes, customer interactions, and quote status changes at a glance. The activity feed shows:

  • Quotes you've created
  • Quotes that have been viewed by customers
  • Quotes that have been accepted or declined
  • Invoices that have been paid

💬 Quote Creation

Tap the microphone button and describe the job in your own words. Quoted transcribes your voice and generates a professional quote in seconds.

Tips for better quotes:

  • Mention the customer name and location
  • Describe the scope of work clearly
  • Include specific materials if relevant
  • Mention quantities and measurements

Works on mobile too - quote from your truck on the job site!

Prefer typing? No problem. You can type your job description instead of speaking. The AI works the same way with text input.

Use text input when you're in a noisy environment or prefer to be precise with your wording.

Quoted's AI uses your Pricing Brain to generate accurate estimates based on:

  • Your hourly labor rate
  • Your material markup percentage
  • Your minimum job amount
  • Patterns learned from your previous quotes

The more you use Quoted, the smarter it gets at understanding your pricing.

After generating a quote, you can edit any line item:

  • Tap a line item to enter edit mode
  • Adjust the description, quantity, rate, or total
  • Add new line items with the + button
  • Remove items by tapping the delete icon

Every correction teaches the AI. When you edit pricing, Quoted learns your preferences and applies them to future quotes.

Speed up your workflow with keyboard shortcuts (desktop only):

  • Cmd+E or Ctrl+E - Jump to first editable field
  • Cmd+S or Ctrl+S - Save changes
  • Esc - Cancel editing / close modal

📄 Quote Management

Share your quotes with customers in multiple ways:

  • Share Link - Generate a shareable link your customer can view in their browser
  • Email Quote - Send directly from Quoted with a professional email template
  • Download PDF - Save the PDF and attach it to your own email or text

When you share via email or link, Quoted automatically tracks when the quote is viewed.

If you shared a quote outside of Quoted (via text message, in-person PDF, etc.), use the Mark as Sent button to update the quote status.

This ensures your quote tracking is accurate and enables view notifications when the customer opens the link.

Download a professional PDF of any quote. The PDF includes:

  • Your business logo and contact info
  • Customer name and address
  • Itemized line items with quantities and pricing
  • Total and any applicable terms

PDFs are formatted to look great when printed or emailed.

Need to create a similar quote? Use Duplicate to copy an existing quote as a starting point.

Perfect for:

  • Repeat customers with similar jobs
  • Creating variations of a quote (good/better/best pricing)
  • Quickly adjusting for a similar scope of work

Track the status of every quote through its lifecycle:

  • Draft - Quote created but not yet sent
  • Sent - Quote has been shared with customer
  • Viewed - Customer has opened the quote (with view count)
  • Accepted - Customer has accepted the quote
  • Declined - Customer has declined

You'll receive notifications when customers view your quotes, so you know the perfect time to follow up.

Delete quotes you no longer need. Deleted quotes are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Note: If a quote has been sent to a customer, consider keeping it for your records instead of deleting.

Let customers accept quotes directly from the share link with a legally-binding electronic signature:

  • Accept Button - Customer clicks to accept the quote
  • Typed Signature - Customer types their name as their signature
  • Legal Record - We capture name, IP address, and timestamp
  • Instant Notification - You're notified immediately when a quote is accepted

Accepted quotes automatically move to "Won" status and can be converted to invoices with one click.

👥 Customers

Quoted automatically builds a customer database as you create quotes. Each customer profile includes:

  • Name and contact information
  • Address (auto-filled on future quotes)
  • Quote history with that customer
  • Total value of quotes and jobs

When creating a new quote, start typing a customer name and Quoted will suggest matches from your existing customers.

Selecting an existing customer automatically fills in their address and contact info.

View all quotes for a specific customer in one place. Great for:

  • Seeing what you've quoted them before
  • Referencing past pricing for similar work
  • Understanding repeat customer patterns

💳 Invoicing

When a customer accepts a quote, convert it to an invoice with one click. The invoice inherits all line items and customer information from the quote.

You can adjust the invoice before sending if the final scope changed from the original quote.

Track your invoices through their lifecycle:

  • Draft - Invoice created but not sent
  • Sent - Invoice shared with customer
  • Viewed - Customer has opened the invoice
  • Paid - Payment received
  • Overdue - Payment past due date

Mark invoices as paid when you receive payment. Your dashboard shows outstanding invoice totals so you always know what's owed to you.

🧠 AI Learning (Pricing Brain)

Your Pricing Brain learns from every quote you create and every correction you make. It builds a personalized pricing model that understands:

  • How you price different types of work
  • Your typical labor hours for common tasks
  • Your material costs and markup patterns
  • Job complexity factors that affect pricing

The more you use Quoted, the smarter and more accurate it becomes.

Every time you edit a quote, you're teaching the AI. For example:

  • If you consistently increase labor hours for bathroom work, the AI learns to quote bathroom jobs higher
  • If you reduce material costs for a certain product, the AI adjusts future estimates
  • If you add line items the AI missed, it learns to include those next time

Add a note when you make corrections to help the AI understand why.

Each generated quote shows a confidence level indicating how certain the AI is about the pricing. Higher confidence means the AI has seen similar jobs before and is more likely to be accurate.

Lower confidence quotes should be reviewed more carefully - these are jobs the AI is less familiar with.

Configure your baseline pricing in Account > Pricing Brain > Global Settings:

  • Hourly labor rate - Your default rate per hour
  • Material markup - Percentage to add on top of material costs
  • Minimum job amount - Smallest quote you'll accept

These settings form the foundation of your AI pricing model.

View the patterns your AI has learned in the Pricing Brain section. You'll see insights like:

  • Your average pricing for different job types
  • Materials you commonly use
  • Labor hour patterns
  • Pricing adjustments you frequently make

This transparency helps you understand how the AI thinks and spot any patterns you want to adjust.

☑ Tasks & Reminders

Add tasks from your dashboard to keep track of follow-ups, deadlines, and to-dos. Each task can include:

  • Description of what needs to be done
  • Due date
  • Optional link to a related quote or customer

Quoted can automatically create follow-up reminders for quotes that haven't received a response. Never let a hot lead go cold.

Configure reminder timing in your account settings.

Quoted automatically sends personalized follow-up emails to customers who haven't responded to your quotes:

  • Smart Timing - Follow-ups are sent at optimal intervals after the quote is viewed
  • Personalized Content - Each email references the specific quote and customer
  • Professional Templates - Emails match your brand with your business info
  • Automatic Stop - Follow-ups stop when the customer accepts, declines, or you mark the quote closed

Focus on your work while Quoted nurtures your pending quotes.

Mark tasks complete with a single tap. Completed tasks are archived so you can see your history but they don't clutter your active list.

⚙ Account Settings

Upload your business logo to appear on all your quotes and invoices. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG.

For best results, use a logo with a transparent background and at least 200x200 pixels.

Configure your business details that appear on quotes and invoices:

  • Business name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Physical address
  • Website

Set default terms and conditions that appear on all your quotes. Common inclusions:

  • Payment terms (e.g., "50% deposit required")
  • Quote validity period
  • Warranty information
  • Cancellation policy

You can override these on individual quotes when needed.

Manage your subscription from Account > Billing:

  • View current plan and usage
  • Upgrade to annual (save 45%)
  • Update payment method
  • Download invoices
  • Cancel subscription

Earn credits by referring other contractors to Quoted. Find your referral link in Account > Referrals.

When someone signs up with your link and subscribes, you both get a discount on your next billing cycle.
