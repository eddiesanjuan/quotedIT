## Creative Agent 1: Voice Experience Revolution

1) **Real-time transcription preview** (F4 I4 C3 D3 R2)  
Live captions while recording; highlight “low confidence” sections; allow immediate re-record.

2) **Conversational quote-building** (F3 I5 C4 D5 R4)  
AI asks the next best question (“Dimensions?” “Materials?”) and builds a structured scope live.

3) **Voice commands during editing** (F4 I4 C3 D4 R3)  
“Make demo higher”, “Add a line item for permits”, “Change labor rate to 120”.

4) **Noise + volume coaching** (F4 I3 C2 D3 R2)  
UI gives “too quiet / too noisy / clipped” feedback pre-upload; reduces failed transcriptions.

5) **Voice templates** (F4 I4 C3 D4 R3)  
“Use my deck template”; auto-prompts for required fields; consistent line items.

6) **Multi-language quoting** (F3 I4 C4 D4 R3)  
Transcribe + generate in Spanish/English; customer sees bilingual quote.

7) **Quote read-back (audio playback)** (F4 I3 C2 D3 R2)  
AI reads the quote for quick approval; improves “eyes-free” review.

8) **Voice-to-CRM tasks** (F4 I4 C3 D3 R3)  
From voice notes: “call John Friday” → task with due date and reminder.

9) **On-device pre-processing** (F3 I3 C4 D3 R2)  
Chunking, silence trimming, compression; reduces upload size and transcription cost.

10) **“Last job replay”** (F4 I3 C2 D3 R2)  
One tap to base this quote off the last similar job (scope + pricing deltas).

## Platform prerequisites (what to fix first)

Several innovations require foundation work (Phase 1):
- A single “quote generation pipeline” (billing + sanity + persistence + analytics) to prevent bypasses and unify telemetry.
- A real job runner model (single scheduler instance, idempotent jobs) before automation is trustworthy.
- Consistent domain/config plumbing (canonical public base URL used by frontend + email + API).
