# Growth Agent Playbook

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | Purpose | Frequency | Platform |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Educational** | Teach contractors business skills | 2x/week | Blog, LinkedIn |
| **Product** | Showcase features, tips | 1x/week | Twitter, YouTube |
| **Success Stories** | Customer wins | 1x/month | Blog, LinkedIn |
| **Industry News** | Relevant trends | 1x/week | Twitter |

### Content Types

#### 1. Blog Posts
- **Length**: 800-1500 words
- **Tone**: Helpful, practical, not salesy
- **Structure**: Problem → Solution → How-to → CTA
- **SEO**: Target long-tail keywords for contractors

**Example Topics**:
- "How to Price Your First Commercial Paint Job"
- "5 Quotes Every Electrician Should Know How to Write"
- "The Hidden Cost of Slow Quoting (And How to Fix It)"

#### 2. Social Posts
- **Twitter**: Short tips, product updates, industry commentary
- **LinkedIn**: Longer posts, professional insights, success stories
- **Tone**: Conversational, helpful, occasionally witty

**Templates**:
```
[TWITTER TIP]
Quick tip for [trade]:
[actionable advice]
[optional emoji]
#contractors #[trade]

[LINKEDIN POST]
[Hook/question]

[2-3 paragraphs of value]

[Subtle mention of how Quoted helps]

[Call to action or question]
```

#### 3. Email Sequences
- **Welcome**: 3-email onboarding sequence
- **Re-engagement**: For inactive users
- **Feature Announcements**: New feature rollouts

## Growth Tactics

### 1. SEO Content

**Target Keywords**:
- "contractor quote software"
- "quoting app for contractors"
- "voice to quote"
- "[trade] pricing guide" (painters, electricians, etc.)

**On-Page SEO Checklist**:
- [ ] Keyword in title
- [ ] Keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] H2 headers with related terms
- [ ] Meta description (155 chars)
- [ ] Internal links
- [ ] Image alt text

### 2. Social Proof Collection

**When to Ask**:
- After positive support interaction
- After successful quote sent
- After 30 days of active usage
- After upgrade to annual

**Request Template**:
```
Hi [Name],

Glad Quoted is working well for you! Quick favor - would you share a sentence or two about your experience?

Just reply to this email with:
1. Your trade
2. What you like most about Quoted
3. Would you recommend it? (yes/no)

Takes 30 seconds and helps other contractors find us.

Thanks!
Eddie @ Quoted
```

### 3. Referral Program

**Structure** (when launched):
- Referrer: 1 month free
- Referee: 1 month free
- Tracking: Unique referral codes

### 4. Community Building

**Reddit Participation**:
- r/Contractors
- r/Electricians
- r/Plumbing
- Trade-specific subreddits

**Rules**:
- Be genuinely helpful first
- Only mention Quoted if truly relevant
- Never spam or self-promote
- Build reputation over time

### 5. Partner Outreach

**Target Partners**:
- Contractor supply stores
- Trade associations
- Business coaches for contractors
- Accounting services for contractors

**Outreach Template** (Queue for Approval):
```
Subject: Partnership idea for [Company]

Hi [Name],

Quick idea: We make quoting software for contractors (Quoted). Your [customers/members] might find it useful.

Would you be open to a quick chat about how we might work together?

No pitch, just exploring.

Best,
Eddie
Quoted
```

## Content Quality Standards

### Before Publishing (Checklist)

- [ ] Genuinely helpful (not just promotional)
- [ ] Matches brand voice
- [ ] Fact-checked
- [ ] Proofread for grammar/typos
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting
- [ ] Clear CTA if applicable
- [ ] No claims about features we don't have
- [ ] No promises about timelines

### Brand Voice Guidelines

**We Sound Like**:
- A helpful friend who happens to know business
- Efficient but not robotic
- Professional but approachable
- Contractor-friendly (no corporate speak)

**We Don't Sound Like**:
- Aggressive salespeople
- Corporate marketing
- Condescending teachers
- AI-generated filler

### Topics to Avoid

- Controversial political topics
- Competitor bashing (factual comparisons OK)
- Unrealistic income claims
- Legal/tax advice (defer to professionals)

## Campaign Frameworks

### Feature Launch

1. **Pre-Launch** (1 week before)
   - Teaser on social
   - Email to waitlist if applicable

2. **Launch Day**
   - Blog post with details
   - Social announcements
   - Email to all users
   - Update changelog

3. **Post-Launch** (week after)
   - Usage tips
   - Early feedback collection
   - Social proof gathering

### Seasonal Campaigns

| Season | Angle | Content Focus |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Spring | "Busy season prep" | Speed, efficiency |
| Summer | "While you're booked" | Time savings |
| Fall | "Before the slowdown" | Organization |
| Winter | "New year, new systems" | Fresh start |

## Automation Opportunities

### Can Automate
- Scheduling pre-approved posts
- Weekly metric collection
- Content calendar reminders
- Standard engagement responses

### Needs Approval
- Any new content publish
- Outreach emails
- Partnership discussions
- Paid advertising

### Never Automate
- Crisis communication
- Competitor responses
- Legal/policy statements
- Customer testimonial attribution
