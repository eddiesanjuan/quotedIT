# Quoted Product Overview

## What Is Quoted?

Quoted is a voice-to-quote SaaS for contractors. Users describe a job verbally, and AI generates a professional PDF quote using their personalized pricing model.

**URL**: https://quoted.it.com

## Core Value Proposition

- **Speed**: Voice in, quote out. No typing, no spreadsheets.
- **Learning AI**: Prices improve with every quote. The system learns YOUR pricing.
- **Professional Output**: PDF quotes that look like an established business.
- **Mobile-First**: Built for contractors on job sites, not at desks.

## Target Customer

### Primary Persona: "The Solo Contractor"
- Trades: Painters, electricians, plumbers, roofers, landscapers
- Size: 1-5 employees
- Pain: Hates paperwork, loses jobs to slow quotes
- Tech: Uses smartphone for everything
- Pricing: Usually has rough pricing in their head, not documented

### Secondary Persona: "The Growing Business"
- Size: 5-20 employees
- Pain: Inconsistent pricing across team members
- Need: Standardized quotes, brand consistency
- Pricing: Has some documentation, needs centralization

### Quote Management
- **PDF Generation**: Professional, customizable PDFs
- **Email Sharing**: Send directly to customers
- **Link Sharing**: Copy shareable link
- **Edit & Refine**: Adjust any line item

### Learning System
- **Acceptance Learning**: When quotes are sent (no edit), system learns
- **Correction Learning**: When quotes are edited, system learns the correction
- **Category Confidence**: Shows how well-trained each job category is
- **Explanation Panel**: Shows WHY the AI priced something

### Invoicing (DISC-071)
- **Quote-to-Invoice**: Convert accepted quote to invoice
- **Invoice Tracking**: Paid/unpaid status
- **Invoice PDF**: Professional invoice documents

### Onboarding
- **Guided Interview**: Conversational pricing setup
- **Quick Setup**: Skip interview, start quoting
- **Template Import**: Upload existing quote to learn from
